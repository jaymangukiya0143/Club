Safety Measures

We keep every corner clean, organised, and clutter-free

Slipping and tripping are usually caused by scattered objects or spills on the floor. We make sure that your workplace is always clean and fresh. We keep aisles clean, organised, and clutter-free to ensure that nothing is in the way that could hurt.

First Aid Training

Providing our employees with first aid stations goes a long way towards safety and is a practical way to deal with emergencies.

Clearly label designated hazardous zones

Areas, where dangerous equipment is stored are clearly labelled and the walkways are highlighted with the necessary signage.

Servicing our equipments and machinery regularly

Machines live longer with regular maintenance. Risk of cause also decreases when equipments are serviced regularly.

Fire Safety

Fire extinguisher cylinder is installed within small distance in whole club.

Covid-19 Safety Measures

All the guidelines given by the goverment are been followed by the club including frequent sanitising, welcoming virtual meeting, digital payments only, face recognition attendance, etc.
